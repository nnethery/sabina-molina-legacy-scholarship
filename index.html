The Sabina Molina Legacy Scholarship

Empowering Hispanic women to pursue higher education and follow their dreams

In memory of Sabina Molina—an incredible grandmother who lived a life of love, kindness, and unwavering commitment to others—this scholarship honors her legacy by supporting a Hispanic high school student who embodies similar values. Sabina's dedication to her family and community continues to inspire us, and this award is designed to empower a young woman who demonstrates compassion, intellectual curiosity, drive, care for others, and a desire to be the change in the world.

This scholarship is awarded to a student who not only achieves academic excellence but also lives out these core principles of compassion, intellectual curiosity, drive, care for others, and a desire to be the change.

This scholarship provides financial support to help alleviate educational expenses and allow the recipient to focus on her studies, community involvement, and personal growth. Applicants are invited to share their stories and examples of how they have embodied these values, reflecting the spirit of Sabina Molina.

By supporting emerging leaders, the Sabina Molina Legacy Scholarship not only preserves her memory but also encourages future generations to lead with integrity and purpose.
